A Vision Rooted in Legacy

Trusted Since 1957

Founded by Dharam Chand Aggarwal in Chandigarh, our family legacy began in 1957 with the wholesale trading of stainless-steel utensils and crockery. This cornerstone of trust has served generations.

Frankvin is the modern expansion of this commitment. We manufacture premium, custom-made modular kitchens and furniture with the speed, precision, and durability our family name has represented for decades.
